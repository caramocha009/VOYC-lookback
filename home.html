Two small group discussion sessions hitting on the milestone of turning 30. identified that those under 25 have a long, short-term goal list, they feel they needs completed by 30. Those who were 27 to 32 years old had fewer longer-term goals. Then we tested if #adulting would be a good social media marketing campaign and found out it does not resonate well. Scratch #adulting.
